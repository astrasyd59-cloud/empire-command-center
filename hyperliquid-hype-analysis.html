I. HYPE Staking Mechanics & Fee Discounts

A. Direct Answers to Client Questions

Client Q&A — Account Structure & Fee Benefits

Q1: If I stake 10 HYPE on my staking account, will the fee discount apply when I trade on that same account?

YES. The fee discount applies to the account where HYPE is staked. If you stake 10+ HYPE (minimum "Wood" tier), you receive a 5% discount on trading fees for all trades executed on that account. The discount is automatically applied at the fee calculation level.

Q2: Can I link a separate trading account to my staking account so the linked account also gets the reduced fees?

YES — via Account Linking Feature. Hyperliquid introduced account linking (May 5, 2025) specifically for this use case. You can designate one account as your "staking account" and link it to a separate "trading account." The trading account will inherit the fee tier/discounts from the staking account.

Important Constraints: (1) Account linking is PERMANENT and cannot be undone; (2) Only one trading account can be linked per staking account; (3) The feature must be enabled via the testnet interface first; (4) Both accounts must be under your control (you cannot link someone else's account).

B. Staking Strategy for Fee Optimization

For active traders on Hyperliquid, the staking tier system creates a clear ROI calculation:

Break-Even Analysis: Staking for Fee Discounts

Scenario: Trader executing $1M/month volume with 0.05% taker fees = $500/month base fees.

  • Wood Tier (10 HYPE): 5% discount = $25/month savings. At $220 staking value = 136% annualized ROI on staking cost
  • Bronze Tier (1,000 HYPE): 10% discount = $50/month savings. At $22,000 staking value = 2.7% annualized ROI (plus 2.26% staking yield = ~5% total)
  • Silver Tier+: Only justified for $10M+/month volume traders or long-term HYPE holders

Recommendation: For most traders, the Wood tier (10 HYPE) offers optimal risk/reward. Higher tiers make sense only for: (1) Very high volume traders ($10M+/month); (2) Long-term HYPE believers seeking governance rights; (3) Institutional accounts managing multiple sub-accounts.

C. Account Structure Best Practices

Based on Hyperliquid's account linking feature, sophisticated traders should consider this structure:

  1. Staking Account: Hold HYPE for fee tier (minimum 10 HYPE for Wood). Keep minimal trading activity here to avoid exposure of staked funds.
  2. Trading Account: Link to staking account to inherit fee discounts. This is your active trading wallet with operational funds only.
  3. Cold Storage: Majority of HYPE holdings in hardware wallet, unstaked. Only stake what's needed for fee tier.

Risk Note: Account linking is permanent. Ensure you control both accounts and have backed up private keys before linking.
