## Telegram-Specific Tips

✅ **DO:**
- Test at 64x64px (chat list size)
- Keep main element in center 70% (circular crop)
- Use strong contrast for tiny size readability
- Consider dark mode users (70% of Telegram users)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use thin lines (<2px at 512px scale)
- Put critical detail at edges (gets cropped)
- Use subtle gradients that disappear at small sizes
- Overcomplicate with too many elements

---

## Recommended Workflow

1. **Generate all 5** using the detailed prompts
2. **Test at 64x64** (resize and check clarity)
3. **Get feedback** on 2-3 favorites
4. **Iterate** on the winner with variations
5. **Export** at 512x512 PNG for Telegram
6. **Create variants** for light/dark modes if needed
