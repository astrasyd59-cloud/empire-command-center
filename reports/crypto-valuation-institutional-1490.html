How to Value Crypto vs Stocks:
Unlike stocks, crypto has no earnings or cash flows (mostly). Instead we use:

• MC/FDV Ratio: Market Cap ÷ Fully Diluted Value. Lower = more supply inflation risk. < 0.5 is concerning.
• Float/Turnover: Daily volume ÷ Circulating supply. Higher = more liquid, efficient price discovery.
• Fee/Market Cap: Annualized fees ÷ Market Cap (DeFi only). > 10% is exceptional value.
• Network Value/Tx: Market Cap ÷ Daily transactions. Lower = cheaper per unit of utility.
