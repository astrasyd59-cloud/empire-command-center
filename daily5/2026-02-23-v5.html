Price-to-Earnings Ratio (Chapter 41: Equity Valuation)

P/E = Market Price / Earnings Per Share
P/E = $264.58 / $7.91 = 33.45x

Interpretation: Investors pay $33.45 for every $1 of AAPL earnings. This is below the S&P 500 Tech sector average of ~36x, suggesting relative value.
