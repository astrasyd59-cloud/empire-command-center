# Asset-Macro Correlations

How macro factors drive each instrument in the ASTRA universe.

## Gold (GC=F)

### Primary Drivers

| Factor | Correlation | Impact |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| DXY | Strong negative | DXY up → Gold down |
| Real yields (10Y - inflation) | Strong negative | Rising real yields → Gold down |
| VIX | Weak positive | High volatility → safe haven bid |
| Fed policy | Direct | Dovish = bullish, Hawkish = bearish |

### Trading Notes

- **DXY strengthening** = headwind for Gold. Size down on longs.
- **Real yields rising** = Gold's opportunity cost increases.
- **Safe haven flows** = Gold catches a bid when stocks crash.
- **Central bank buying** = structural support (China, Russia, Turkey).

### Key Levels to Watch

- $5,000 psychological
- $5,200 recent highs
- $4,800 support zone

## Silver (SI=F)

### Primary Drivers

| Factor | Correlation | Impact |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Gold | Strong positive | Silver follows Gold, amplified |
| Industrial demand | Medium | Solar, electronics, EVs |
| Gold/Silver ratio | Mean reversion | Above 90 = Silver cheap |
| DXY | Negative | Same as Gold but more volatile |

### Trading Notes

- **More volatile than Gold** — ATR is bigger, stops wider.
- **Industrial metal + precious metal** — dual personality.
- **When Gold/Silver ratio > 90** — Silver catch-up trade potential.
- **Solar demand** = structural tailwind.

### Key Levels

- $30 psychological
- $32 resistance
- $28 support

---

## S&P 500 E-mini (ES=F)

### Primary Drivers

| Factor | Correlation | Impact |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| VIX | Strong negative | VIX up → ES down |
| 10Y yields | Negative | Rising yields = pressure on multiples |
| Fed expectations | Direct | Rate cuts = bullish |
| Earnings season | Direct | beats/misses move market |
| DXY | Weak positive | Strong USD can hurt multinationals |

### Trading Notes

- **VIX > 25** = elevated fear, reduce size on longs.
- **VIX < 15** = complacency, watch for shocks.
- **10Y yields rising fast** = growth stock pain.
- **Fed pivot** = best ES rallies happen here.

### Key Levels

- 6,000 psychological
- 5,800 major support
- 6,200 resistance

## Nasdaq 100 E-mini (NQ=F)

### Primary Drivers

| Factor | Correlation | Impact |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| 10Y yields | Very negative | Tech = long duration, yield-sensitive |
| VIX | Strong negative | Growth stocks hate volatility |
| MAG7 earnings | Direct | NVDA, AAPL, MSFT drive index |
| Fed policy | Direct | Rate cuts = NQ rallies hard |

### Trading Notes

- **More yield-sensitive than ES** — when 10Y moves, NQ moves more.
- **NVDA is the index** — single stock can move entire NQ.
- **Growth vs value** — NQ = pure growth play.

### Key Levels

- 25,000 psychological
- 24,000 support
- 26,000 resistance

## AUD/USD

### Primary Drivers

| Factor | Correlation | Impact |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| DXY | Strong negative | AUD is anti-dollar |
| Iron ore prices | Medium | Australia's #1 export |
| China growth | Medium | Australia's #1 customer |
| RBA rate decisions | Direct | Rate hikes = AUD bullish |
| Risk sentiment | Positive | AUD is risk-on currency |

### Trading Notes

- **Commodity currency** — tracks iron ore, coal, gold.
- **China proxy** — when China data strong, AUD rallies.
- **Risk-on/risk-off** — AUD sells off in fear, rallies in greed.
- **Carry trade** — used in risk-on carry strategies.

### Key Levels

- 0.70 psychological
- 0.65 major support
- 0.75 resistance

## GBP/USD (Cable)

### Primary Drivers

| Factor | Correlation | Impact |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| DXY | Negative | Cable is anti-dollar |
| BOE rate decisions | Direct | Rate hikes = GBP bullish |
| UK economic data | Direct | GDP, inflation, employment |
| Brexit news | Event risk | Still causes volatility |
| Risk sentiment | Medium | GBP benefits from risk-on |

### Trading Notes

- **BOE meetings** = key volatility events.
- **Less volatile than AUD** — moves slower, cleaner trends.
- **London session** = most liquid, best spreads.

### Key Levels

- 1.30 psychological
- 1.25 support
- 1.35 resistance

## Macro Events That Move Everything

| Event | Frequency | Impact |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| FOMC | 8x/year | Everything moves |
| NFP | Monthly | USD, yields, equities |
| CPI | Monthly | Inflation expectations |
| GDP | Quarterly | Growth outlook |
| ECB/BOE/RBA | Monthly | Currency pairs |

**Rule:** No new trades 24h before FOMC, NFP, or CPI.
