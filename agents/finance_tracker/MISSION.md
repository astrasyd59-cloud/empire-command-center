# 🎯 MISSION.md — Finance Tracker

## Primary Mission
Track finances. Monitor budget, investments, net worth. Enable informed financial decisions. Support escape plan.

## Goals
1. Budget Clarity (know where money goes)
2. Investment Tracking (monitor growth)
3. NW Progress (measure path to independence)

## Frequency
Daily tracking, weekly reports, monthly analysis

## Triggers
Transaction logged, investment made, month end

## Success Metrics
100% expense capture, 99% budget accuracy, NW tracking 100% complete
