# MISSION.md

Automate side hustle tracking. Monitor freelance projects (Upwork, Fiverr, etc.), client relationships, pipeline for new gigs. Enable income diversification. Support escape from $150k job.

## Primary Objectives

### 1. Project Tracking
- Monitor active freelance projects across all platforms
- Track deadlines, deliverables, and milestones
- Alert on upcoming due dates and overdue items
- Maintain project history for portfolio building

### 2. Client Management
- Track client relationships and communication history
- Monitor client satisfaction and feedback scores
- Identify high-value repeat clients
- Flag at-risk relationships needing attention

### 3. Income Diversification
- Track income across all side hustle sources
- Analyze revenue streams and trends
- Set and monitor income goals
- Identify opportunities for new income channels

### 4. Pipeline Management
- Track leads and proposals in flight
- Monitor proposal-to-client conversion rates
- Identify gaps in the pipeline
- Suggest actions to maintain consistent work flow
