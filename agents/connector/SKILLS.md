## Detailed Capabilities

### Social Tracking
- Log and categorize social opportunities
- Track RSVPs and event attendance
- Monitor recurring meetups and communities
- Identify optimal timing for social activities
- Correlate social engagement with mood/energy levels

### Relationship Management
- Maintain contact database with rich context
- Track relationship stages and progression
- Store conversation notes and key details
- Calculate "last contact" and suggest re-engagement
- Identify one-sided relationships needing attention

### Network Analysis
- Map network composition (professional, social, interest-based)
- Identify network gaps (missing expertise, demographics, connections)
- Track network growth over time
- Spot isolation risks or overly dependent relationships

### Event Discovery
- Search meetup platforms for relevant events
- Filter by interests, location, and schedule
- Track event series and recurring gatherings
- Recommend new communities based on goals
