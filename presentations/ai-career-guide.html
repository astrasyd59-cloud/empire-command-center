05

Should You Learn Math?

"Prioritize based on decisions you need to make."

Deep Learning is Emerging

Understanding gradient descent, momentum, Adam optimizer helps you debug when things break.

As Tools Mature

Less math depth needed. Auto-differentiation handles calculus now.

Learn for curiosity — if it interests you, dive in regardless of immediate utility.

06

Scoping Successful AI Projects

Step 1: Identify Business Problems

Ask domain experts: "What are the top 3 things you wish worked better?"

Step 2: Brainstorm AI Solutions

Don't execute the first idea — find the BEST idea.

Step 3: Assess Feasibility & Value

Check published work, build quick proof-of-concept.

Step 4: Determine Milestones

ML metrics (accuracy) + Business metrics (revenue, engagement).

Step 5: Budget Resources

Data, personnel, time, integrations needed.

08

"Ready, Fire, Aim"

Ready, Aim, Fire

Plan carefully, validate thoroughly. Use when execution cost is HIGH and study can shed light on value.

Ready, Fire, Aim ✓

Jump in, execute, discover problems quickly. Use when execution cost is LOW and iteration is cheap.

For ML models: Build end-to-end systems quickly and revise until they work well.
09

Job Search Strategy

Don't switch both role AND industry at the same time.

Same Industry Switch Industry
Same Role Easiest ✓ Moderate
Switch Role Moderate Hardest

Startups

Easier to switch roles (wear multiple hats). Help with AI even if not your job.

Big Companies

Rigid reward systems. Harder to move outside your scope.

10

Informational Interviews

Your secret weapon for unknown territory.

Job titles are inconsistent. Data Scientist = slides at Company A, production code at Company B.
  • ✓ What do you do in a typical week?
  • ✓ What skills are most important for success?
  • ✓ How does your team work together?
  • ✓ What is the hiring process?
  • ✓ What made standout candidates shine?
11

Finding the Right Job

  • ✓ Fundamentals: Compelling resume, project portfolio, strong interview prep
  • ✓ Proceed Respectfully: Win-win mindset, exit gracefully from current roles
  • ✓ Choose Teammates Carefully: You become like the people you work with
  • ✓ Get Community Help: Friends/mentors provide advice, inside knowledge, referrals
The job search is one small step — not the ultimate goal. Focus on the decades-long journey.
