# HEARTBEAT.md - Proactive Checks

Run these checks during heartbeat polls (rotate through, 2-4x per day):

## Checklist

- [ ] **Inbox** — Any urgent unread messages?
- [ ] **Calendar** — Events in next 24-48h?
- [ ] **Notion** — Any tasks due today?
- [ ] **Trading** — Positions to review? Journal entries pending?

## When to Alert

- Important email arrived
- Calendar event coming up (<2h)
- Trading opportunity or risk
- Been >8h since last contact

## When to Stay Quiet

- Late night (23:00-08:00) unless urgent
- Human clearly busy
- Just checked <30 min ago
